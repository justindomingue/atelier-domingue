## Selvedge Jeans 1873 — source audit fixes

### Fly geometry (the big one — `94421ee`)
- **Front CF outline:** 7'→8 straight, crotch curve from pt8. `fly_end` (pt8 + 1″ along fly-dir) is a dashed construction reference only — not on the cut outline.
- **Crotch curve:** cubic Bezier, P1 along fly-dir for tangent continuity at pt8 (14° → 1° kink).
- **SA_FLY (¾″)** wraps ~1″ along the crotch curve to the fly terminus before narrowing to SA_CROTCH (⅜″). Fly-stop notch at terminus.
- **1873 fly piece:** `fly_height = |7'−fly_end|` (the fly-line length). Bottom curve at `|8−fly_end|` from fold bottom. Notch at curve_start.
- **One-piece fly:** `fly_extension = |7'−fly_end|`. Source says "the little fly extension" — our `jeans_fly_instructions.md` transcription had wrongly added "(rise curve from 1′ to 7′)", which is the waist edge. Corrected.
- **Naming trap:** `curves['rise']` is the WAIST curve (1'→7' across the top), not the CF-rise despite the name. Comment added.
- **CF length formula:** `body_rise − seat/12`. Scales with rise — low-rise inputs yield short flies.

### Fly overlay (`1f92f91`)
- Red construction-reference outline on the front panel showing fly attachment position.
- Fold edge along CF (7'→fly_end), outer edge 1.75″ into panel running up to the waist curve, bottom curve at fly terminus. Open at top.

### Other jeans fixes
- **Yoke grain** (`1bc6234`): side-seam parallel (user preference over source's ⊥-to-center).
- **Waistband width** (`1bc6234`): 3.0 → 3.375″ (adds ⅜″ inside-turn for selvedge wrap).
- **Back pocket cut_count** (`1bc6234`): 2 → 1 for 1873 variant (historically single right pocket).
- **Back pocket depth errata** (`1ed159b`): source text says 7″/8″, diagram shows 6″/7″ — diagram is correct. Documented in `jeans_back_pocket_errata.md`.
- **Pocket-opening/sideseam SA gap** (`690505a`): `hip_below_facing` could be a degenerate 1-point edge when pocket_lower coincides with pt4. Skip from `sa_edges` when `len < 2` so the opening miters against the next edge.

## MM&S Trouser Block — PDF audit fixes

### Core geometry (`e86b5b0`)
- **D-3 (critical):** waist_side_x now parametric on Wbg: `cf_waist_x − (¼Wbg + pleat − relocation)`. Was hardcoded 1.5.
- **D-4:** hip curve starts at crotch/hip midpoint, not hipline.
- **D-1/D-2 reverted** (`34cb85c`): interpolated crotch point + CF-anchored guide give a cleaner J-curve than the PDF's direct placement. Intentional deviation, commented.

### Curves
- **Hip curve** (`1bd4ae7`): quadratic Bezier — inflection-free by construction. Bulge scales with `waist_side_x`.
- **Tangent continuity** (`3b929f5`): side_upper built before hip so hip can mirror its tangent at mid_side.
- **Hem SA** (`34cb85c`): split U-shape into 3 edges so vertical perpendicular drops get 1.5 cm leg-SA, not the 4 cm turn-up. Front and back.

### EaseConfig (`e86b5b0`, `9c080d1`)
- Frozen dataclass: `cw_reduction`, `btw_ease`, `creaseline_shift`, `cb_slant`, `cb_height_extra`, `sideseam_intake_target`, `hip_verify_ease`.
- Presets: `slim` / `standard` / `relaxed` under `mms_ease:` YAML key. Bare name or `{preset: X, ...overrides}`.
- `cw_reduction` and `hip_verify_ease` are `float | None` — None defers to per-variant `PLEAT_CONFIGS` defaults.

### Per-variant tuning (`527c310`, `10f76ae`)
- `cw_reduction` in `PLEAT_CONFIGS`: dart=4.5, pleated=4.0 (matches PDF per-variant charts).
- `hip_verify_range`: dart/1-pleat=(2.5,3.5), 2-pleat=(3.0,4.0).

### Waist intake auto-adapt (`fc5ed3b`)
- PDF p.22 "Pattern Adaptation": 1.5 cm is a hard cap ("intake max"), not soft target.
- Cap `waist_side_x` at 1.5 cm; widen primary pleat (or dart) by the excess. Warn if excess > 2 cm ("only to some extent — major changes at basic block").
- Size-50: raw 2.5 → capped 1.5, pleat 3.5→4.5 cm.

### 0-pleat variant (`5147e70`)
- Is MM&S "Trousers with Dart" (pp. 30–33), not flat-front.
- Added 2 cm front dart (9.5 cm long, centred on creaseline) + 0.5 cm CF waist taper.
- `sideseam_relocation` 2.0 → 1.0. Renamed `'Flat-Front'` → `'Dart'` / `'Trouser with Dart (MM&S)'`.

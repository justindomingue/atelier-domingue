# Pattern Text Errata

Errors and ambiguities found in the "Drafting the Front" lesson from Historical Tailoring Masterclasses.

Source: https://masterclasses.historical-tailoring.com/lesson/drafting-the-front-4/

## 1. "Square up from 5 to find points 8 and 9"

**Section:** Marking Out the Widths

**Text says:** "Square up from 5 to find points 8 and 9."

**Problem:** Two issues:
- "Square up" is misleading — it should be "square left" (i.e., draw a horizontal line from point 5 toward the waist). Point 8 is where this horizontal meets the vertical dropped from point 4.
- "Points 8 and 9" is incorrect — point 9 is defined separately in the Point Adjustments section ("Draw a line at 45 degrees from point 5. Point 9 is half the distance of points 5 to 6."). The text likely means "points 7 and 8," since point 7 is the drop from point 1 on the same horizontal as 5 and 8.

## 2. Direction of 45-degree line for point 9

**Section:** Point Adjustments

**Text says:** "Draw a line at 45 degrees from point 5."

**Problem:** The direction of the 45-degree line is not specified. It goes **down-left** from point 5 (toward the 7/8 side), not down-right toward the inseam. This matters because point 9 needs to sit on the crotch curve path from 8 through 9 to 6.

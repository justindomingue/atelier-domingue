# Pattern Piece Annotations

Concise notes to print on or alongside each pattern piece. Organized per piece, with seam allowances per edge, key construction reminders, and pressing directions.

---

## FRONT LEG

**Layout:** Cut on selvedge (side seam on selvedge edge). Trace one, flip, trace the second — do not cut doubled. Mark hem, knee, crotch, hip lines in chalk (no notches).

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Side seam | 3/4" | Lay directly on selvedge to prevent fraying |
| Inseam | 3/8" | Back leg has 3/4" — front aligns to back's 3/8" mark |
| Fly/crotch | 3/8" | Stretch curve in SA only with steam before folding |
| Waist | 3/8" | |
| Pocket opening | 3/8" | Stretch curve in SA after sewing pocket bag |
| Hem | 7/8" | Two 3/8" folds + 1/8" for turn width |

**Fly extension (left leg):** Fold back 3/8", then fold entire fly edge another 3/8". Gently stretch curve. Mark bottom of fly extension on right side.

**Fly extension (right leg — 1873/one-piece):** Mark 3/8" from edge, trim off extension, clip 3/8" at bottom.

**Pocket opening:** Set pocket bag back 1/8" from edge so bag isn't visible from right side. Top stitch 1/8" from edge (1873: single row; modern: add second row at 3/8").

**Side seam at pocket:** Slide front leg forward ~1/8" from facing at the pocket — gives ease for hand entry and curve around body.

**Rivet positions:** Top of pocket opening (~1/4" from waistband), bottom of pocket opening, top of watch pocket.

## BACK LEG

**Layout:** Cut on selvedge (side seam on selvedge). Same trace-and-flip method as front.

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Side seam | 3/4" | On selvedge |
| Inseam | 3/4" | Front aligns to 3/8" marked line on back |
| Seat seam | 5/8" (1873) or 3/8" overlap (modern) | Stretch curve in SA only |
| Yoke seam | 3/8" | **Modern only:** trim this 3/8" off before sewing |
| Waist | 3/8" | |
| Hem | 7/8" | Same as front |

**Yoke seam (1873):** Distribute fullness from back leg evenly along yoke. Sew with leg on bottom (feed dogs help). Press seam toward leg (away from yoke). Iron on seam only — don't press out fullness.

**Yoke seam (modern):** Overlap leg onto yoke (both right sides up) touching the 3/8" line. Sew 3/16" from edge. Fold, press, top stitch 1/16" then 5/16" from fold.

**Seat seam:** After sewing, stretch crotch curve in SA only until it lies straight. Press open (1873: press to left side). Hip line should be straight across after pressing — if not, check your sewing.

**Crotch point:** Taper inward up to 1/4" with hip curve if there's a point. Smooth transition matters.

**Inseam:** If front leg is longer, gently stretch back inseam to match. Sew hem-to-hem through crotch in one pass. Modern: press back SA over front ~3/8", top stitch 3/8" from seam. 1873: press SA toward front, top stitch 1/8" from seam.

---

## YOKE

**Orientation:** Tall/long end = seat seam. Short end = side seam. Don't reverse this!

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Bottom (back leg seam) | 3/8" | Modern: mark this line, back leg overlaps to it |
| Seat seam | matches back leg | Align with back leg seat seam |
| Side seam | matches back leg | |
| Waist | 3/8" | |

**1873 straight yoke — pleat:** Mark two dart points at top. Fold closer mark (near selvedge) to meet the farther mark. Stitch across 1/4" from waist edge (hidden under waistband).

**Top stitch spacing:** 1/16"–1/8" from seam edge (first row). Keep this distance consistent for all top stitching throughout the entire project.

---

## WAISTBAND

**Layout:** Cut on fold if possible (for muslin); on selvedge for denim.

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Bottom (waist seam) | 3/8" | |
| Top | fold line 1 1/2" from waist seam | |
| Ends (at fly) | square to waistband, ~2" beyond fly | Trim after fitting |

**Fold line:** 1 1/2" from waist seam = top of waistband. Fold marks slightly to inside.

**Ends:** Square a line at each fly edge (don't follow fly angle). Leave ~2" for buttonhole/button, trim excess. Press ends in, keep them square.

**Top stitch:** 1/8" from waist seam, catching selvedge underneath (verify 1/4"–3/8" overlap before sewing). Two passes to avoid sewing with full jeans under machine arm.

**Buttonhole position:** Center of waistband (3/4" from top and bottom), 1/2" from end, 3/4" wide, ~1/8" wider than button.

**Suspender button positions:** First aligned with top of front pocket, second ~2 1/2" away, each 1/2" below waistband top. Back: two points ~2" from center back.

## FLY PIECE (One-Piece)

**Dimensions:** Double the fly length + 2", about 4" wide (1 3/4" finished × 2 + 1/2" SA). Ideally on selvedge.

| Edge | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| Raw long edge | Fold 1/2" to wrong side |
| Selvedge long edge | Fold over raw edge, set back 1/16" behind selvedge |
| Across middle | Fold and press (creates the fly crease) |

**Buttonhole positions:** Lower buttonhole 2"–2 1/4" above fold. Space evenly to top. Outer edge 3/8" from fold. Inner edge ~1/8" wider than buttons (3/4" typical). Buttonholes should be 1/8" larger than buttons.

---

## FLY PIECE (1873 Two-Piece)

**Fold:** Press in half, wrong sides together, top edge set back 1/16" from selvedge. Baste along open edge.

**Inlay marks at top:** 3/8" left of inlay = seam line (waistband). 3/8" right of inlay + 3/4" further = waistband buttonhole.

**Buttonhole layout:** Same spacing rules as one-piece. Lower hole 2"–2 1/2" from bottom. Outer edge 3/8" from fold. 1 1/2"–2 1/2" spacing between holes.

---

## BUTTON EXTENSION (1873 fly)

**Dimensions:** 4 1/4" wide × (fly extension length + 3"). Width = 1 3/4" × 2 + two 3/8" SA.

**Bottom shaping:** 1/2" from one end, draw 30° line sloping toward fold. Sew, trim SA to 1/4" tapering to 1/8" at fold. Turn out.

**Attachment:** Align 3 raw edges with right leg. Bottom mark 1" from extension end, line up with clip. Sew 3/8" SA from waist to clip.

## BACK POCKET

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Top | 7/8" (pocket) / 7/8" (lining) | Fold top, tuck raw edge under = finished 3/8" |
| Sides | 3/8" (pocket) / 1/2" (lining) | Lining trimmed smaller |
| Bottom | 3/8" (pocket) / 1/2" (lining) | Lining trimmed smaller |

**Placement on back leg:** 2 1/2" from selvedge edge, 2" below yoke seam. Right side of pocket at that intersection. Left side rotated to ~1 1/2" below yoke seam.

**Top stitch pocket top:** 1873: single row 3/8" from edge. Modern: two rows (1/8" from edge + 1/4"–3/8" from first row, matching yoke spacing).

**Sewing to leg:** Start from selvedge side. First row 1/8" from edge + back tack. Before sewing the last side, slide top corner toward selvedge ~1/8" for seat ease. Second row tapers from 5/8" at top corners to 1/4" from stitching at 2" below pocket opening.

**Thread ends:** Leave long, pull through to wrong side, tie knots, trim to 1/2".

**Rivet:** At top of each back pocket.

## FRONT POCKET FACING

| Edge | Seam Allowance |
|------|---------------|
| Bottom & inner edges | 3/8" |
| Curved waist & side seam | no marking needed |

**Top stitch facing to pocket bag:** 1/16"–1/8" from edge along inner and bottom edges.

## WATCH POCKET

| Edge | Seam Allowance | Lining SA |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| Top | 7/8" | 7/8" |
| Sides | 3/8" | 1/2" |
| Bottom | 3/8" | 1/2" |

**Top edge options:** Standard (no selvedge), selvedge folded under, or SA replaced with selvedge strip.

**Top stitch:** 1/16" from top edge + 3/8" from top (match yoke spacing).

**Placement (modern):** On right pocket facing, 1"–1 1/2" below top edge, 1 1/2" from side seam, following waist angle. Top stitch 1/16"–1/8" from edge + second row 3/8" from first (taper to 1/2" at top).

**Placement (1873):** After waistband. Top extends midway up waistband, bottom just below front pocket opening. Top stitch 1/8" from edge + 1/4" from first row.

## POCKET BAG

**Layout:** Cut on fold as marked.

**Pocket opening:** Trace from front leg pattern onto bag, cut out. Sew 3/8" SA. Stretch curve in SA with steam.

**Closing the bag:** Fold in half (inside out, facing outside). Sew bottom + curved edge to side seam. Diagonal stitch at corners (45°) instead of sharp turn. Clip corners. Top stitch 1/8"–1/4" from edge.

**Top closure:** Sew across waist seam 1/4" from edge through all layers.

---

## BELT LOOPS (Modern)

**Dimensions:** 4" minimum per loop, 1 1/2" wide strip (1/2" finished). Ideally on selvedge.

**Preparation:** Press raw edge under ~1/2", fold selvedge over = 1/2" wide. Top stitch 1/16" from each long edge.

**Lower attachment (before waistband):** Right sides together on waist mark, at right angles. Bar tack 1/4" from waist.

**Upper attachment (after waistband):** Bar tack 3/8"–1/2" below waist. Fold up. Bar tack 1/8" from bottom. Fold top 1/8" above waistband top (ease for belt). Bar tack across top.

---

## BACK CINCH BELT (1873)

**Layout:** Cut on selvedge, mirror for other side.

**Sewing:** Press SA on ends, press raw edge, fold selvedge over everything. Sew along center line end to end.

**Buckle:** On left belt, slide on buckle, overlap ~1" and top stitch (box stay).

**Attachment:** Center on waistband seam (vertical) and center back (horizontal). Box stays at each end, 1/8" from belt edges, with vertical lines 1" from each end.

---

## HEM ALLOWANCE (all leg pieces)

**Total allowance:** 7/8" (= two 3/8" folds + 1/8" turn width).

**Sewing:** Fold to wrong side, fold raw edge under, sew 3/8" from wrong side. Use seam jumper at thick crossings.

**Turn-up:** ~1 1/2" on outside. Press crease. Wait until jeans are fully complete before finalizing.

---

## UNIVERSAL TOP-STITCH SPACING REFERENCE

Establish your spacing with the yoke seam and keep it consistent everywhere:

| Context | First Row | Second Row |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Yoke seam | 1/16"–1/8" from edge | 5/16"–3/8" from first row |
| Seat seam | match yoke | match yoke |
| Inseam (modern) | 3/8" from seam | optional 1/16"–1/8" from seam |
| Inseam (1873) | 1/8" from seam | — |
| Side seam | 1/8" from seam | — |
| Pocket top (1873) | 3/8" from edge | — |
| Pocket top (modern) | 1/8" from edge | match yoke spacing |
| Fly curve | match yoke | ~1/8" inside first row |
| Waistband | 1/8" from seam | along top edge |
| Back pocket to leg | 1/8" from edge | 1/4" (1873) or match yoke (modern) |
| Watch pocket | 1/16"–1/8" from edge | 3/8" from first (modern) or 1/4" (1873) |
| Belt loop edges | 1/16" from edge | — |

## PRESSING DIRECTION CHEAT SHEET

| Seam | Press Toward |
|------|-------------|
| Yoke (1873) | Down toward leg (away from yoke) |
| Yoke (modern) | Fold into yoke, then back to final position |
| Seat seam (1873) | Toward left side |
| Seat seam (modern) | Fold, crease, hammer at yoke junction |
| Inseam (modern) | Back SA over front ~3/8" |
| Inseam (1873) | Toward front leg |
| Side seam | Open (below pocket); pocket area toward back |
| Waistband | Up toward top of waistband |
| Fly extension | 3/8" fold, then another 3/8" fold |
| Crotch curve | Stretch in SA only before folding/pressing |
| Hem | Toward wrong side, raw edge folded under |

**General rule:** Keep iron on the seam itself. Don't press past the SA into the garment body — especially at the yoke (preserves built-in fullness) and crotch curves (preserves stretch).
